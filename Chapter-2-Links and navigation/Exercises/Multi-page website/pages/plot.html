Plot

Jessie follows Jessie Prescott, a young woman from Texas who moves to New York City to pursue her dreams of acting. She ends up becoming a nanny for the wealthy Ross family, taking care of four unique children: Emma, Luke, Ravi, and Zuri. Each episode features comedic adventures, life lessons, and the heartwarming bond that forms between Jessie and the kids as they navigate growing up in the big city.
