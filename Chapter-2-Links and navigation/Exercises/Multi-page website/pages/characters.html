Main Characters

  • Jessie Prescott: A bright, ambitious young woman with a big heart and big dreams.
  • Emma Ross: The eldest child, stylish and a bit of a diva.
  • Luke Ross: The prankster of the family with tons of energy.
  • Ravi Ross: Intelligent and polite, with a pet lizard named Mr. Kipling.
  • Zuri Ross: Sassy and imaginative, often stealing the scene.
  • Bertram: The sarcastic butler who secretly cares about the family.
