JESSIE

Overview | Cast | Reviews | Official Website

Overview

Jessie is an American comedy television series that aired on Disney Channel from September 30, 2011, to October 16, 2015. Created by Pamela Eells O'Connell, the show follows a small-town Texas girl named Jessie Prescott, played by Debby Ryan, who moves to New York City to pursue her dreams of becoming an actress. Instead, she lands a job as a nanny for the wealthy Ross family. The Ross family lives in a luxurious penthouse in a Manhattan skyscraper. The parents, Morgan and Christina Ross, are often away due to their careers in film and fashion, leaving Jessie with the responsibilities.

Cast

  • Jessie (Debby Ryan) takes care of four diverse and spirited children:
  • Emma Ross (Peyton List) – the only biological child, fashion-conscious and social.
  • Luke Ross (Cameron Boyce) – mischievous and flirtatious.
  • Ravi Ross (Karan Brar) – an intelligent boy from India with a pet lizard named Mr. Kipling.
  • Zuri Ross (Skai Jackson) – a witty and imaginative young girl adopted from Uganda.

Reviews

Plugged In recognized its entertainment value but noted it was "a tinge more cynical and a touch more crude" than expected from a typical Disney nanny show.
On IMDb, many users gave positive feedback, with one stating that Jessie is "filled with magic and entertainment" and suitable for all ages.
However, TheTopTens offered a contrasting view, claiming the show "sends out a terrible message to all children".
Similarly, a critical blog post from Kayfil.WordPress.com expressed frustration with the characters’ disrespectful behavior, stating, "the complete lack of respect towards all adults is awful".
These varied perspectives highlight how Jessie appealed to a broad young audience while also raising concerns among more critical viewers.
