Animal Style Fries Recipe

A simple and highly addictive copycat recipe for In-N-Out Animal Style Fries that taste just like the original, loaded French fries!

Ingredients

  • 2 large frozen fries
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 1/2 cup shredded American cheese
  • 1/4 cup mayonnaise
  • 1 tbsp ketchup
  • 1 tbsp sweet relish
  • 1/2 tsp white vinegar
  • 1/2 tsp sugar
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1/4 tsp pepper

Method

  1. Prepare the Fries
    • If using fresh potatoes, peel and cut them into thin fries. Soak in cold water for at least 30 minutes to remove excess starch.
    • Drain and dry completely before frying.
    • Heat oil to 350°F (175°C) and fry in batches until golden brown and crispy. Remove and drain on paper towels, then season with salt.
    • If using frozen fries, bake or air fry them according to package instructions.
  2. Make the Grilled Onions
    • Heat a pan over medium-low heat and add butter or oil.
    • Add chopped onions and cook slowly, stirring occasionally.
    • Add a teaspoon of sugar (optional) to help caramelization.
    • If onions start sticking, add a splash of water. Cook for about 10-15 minutes until they’re golden brown.
  3. Prepare the Sauce
    • In a bowl, mix mayonnaise, ketchup, mustard, relish, vinegar, sugar, and paprika. Stir well and set aside.
  4. Assemble the Fries
    • Place the fries on a serving plate.
    • Lay American cheese slices over hot fries to melt.
    • Top with caramelized onions and drizzle with the sauce.
